Online Bharatanatyam Classes

The same Kalakshetra rigour. The same personal attention. Now in your home, wherever in the world you are.

Not pre-recorded. Not a course platform.

Live classes. Real learning.

Our online classes are live, one-to-one or small-group sessions directly with Guru Smt. Deepa Sadasivam. You receive the same corrections, the same attention, and the same curriculum as our in-person students in Chennai. Nothing is diluted. Nothing is pre-recorded.

Kalpavruksha has been teaching online students since 2020. We have refined the process to the point where students in multiple continents have performed multiple programs after online training with us.

Getting started

How online classes work

📋

1. Enquire

Fill our contact form or WhatsApp us. Tell us your age, dance experience, and goals. We'll suggest the right programme.

🎯

2. Free trial class

Your first session is completely free. We schedule a 60-minute class so you can experience the teaching style with no commitment.

📅

3. Schedule your batch

We work with your timezone to find suitable slots. Flexible scheduling across IST, EST, BST, SGT, and UAE time zones.

💻

4. Begin learning

All you need is Google meet, a 6×6 ft clear space, and a device with a camera. We'll handle everything else.

What you need

Technical requirements

  • 📱
    Device with camera

    Laptop, tablet, or smartphone with a front-facing camera. A laptop placed on a low table (so you can see your whole body) works best.

  • 🌐
    Stable internet connection

    Standard broadband or 4G/5G. We need to see your footwork clearly, so a stable connection is important.

  • 📐
    Clear practice space

    A space of at least 6×6 feet with a hard floor. Remove furniture temporarily if needed. No special flooring required.

  • 🎧
    Headphones or good speakers

    For hearing the nattuvangam (cymbals and vocal) clearly. Wired headphones reduce lag.

  • 📲
    Google meet

    . A free account is sufficient. We'll send you the class link before each session.

Questions about online classes

Many of our online students have matched or exceeded the progress of in-person students.

Yes. We have guided Arangetrams from students who completed their entire preparation online, with occasional in-person intensive sessions in Chennai in the final months. The Arangetram itself typically happens at the student's location, and Guru Deepa can attend in person or help arrange a local nattuvanar.

Online classes follow the exact same curriculum as in-person classes. Sessions are live (not recorded) and typically 60–75 minutes. You'll learn adavus, compositions, abhinaya — everything a Chennai student learns. Sessions are one-to-one or very small groups (2–3 maximum) so you still get personal attention.

We currently offer slots to accommodate IST, SGT, GST, BST/GMT, EST, and PST. Classes are typically early morning IST (which works for Singapore and UAE) and evening IST (which works for UK and parts of the USA). Contact us with your timezone and we will find a slot that works.

Online fees are discussed individually and may vary based on session frequency and batch format (individual vs group). Please contact us for the current fee structure. All fees are paid in advance per term.
