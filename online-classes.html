Online Bharatanatyam Classes

Live, personal, Kalakshetra-style Bharatanatyam via Google Meet — from anywhere in the world.

Not pre-recorded. Not a course platform.

Live classes. Real learning.

Our online classes are live, one-to-one or small-group sessions directly with Guru Smt. Deepa Sadasivam via Google Meet. You receive the same corrections, the same attention, and the same curriculum as our in-person students in Chennai. Nothing is diluted. Nothing is pre-recorded.

Kalpavruksha has been teaching online students since 2020. We currently have students learning from the USA, UK, and across India. Classes focus on regular training and skill development; programme events (Salangai Pooja, Arangetram) are managed in-person as and when the student is ready.

Getting started

How online classes work

📋

1. Enquire

Fill our contact form or WhatsApp us. Tell us your age, dance experience, and goals. We'll suggest the right programme.

🎯

2. Free trial class

Your first session is completely free — a 60-minute class via Google Meet so you can experience the teaching with no commitment.

📅

3. Schedule your batch

We work with your timezone to find suitable slots. Classes meet 2 sessions per week for most batches. Flexible scheduling for IST, EST, BST and other time zones.

💻

4. Begin learning

All you need is Google Meet, a 6×6 ft clear space, and a device with a camera. We'll handle everything else.

What you need

Technical requirements

  • 📱
    Device with camera

    Laptop, tablet, or smartphone with a front-facing camera. A laptop placed on a low table (so you can see your whole body) works best.

  • 🌐
    Stable internet connection

    Standard broadband or 4G/5G. We need to see your footwork clearly, so a stable connection matters.

  • 📐
    Clear practice space

    At least 6×6 feet with a hard floor. No special flooring required.

  • 🎧
    Headphones or good speakers

    For hearing the nattuvangam (cymbals and vocal) clearly. Wired headphones reduce lag.

  • 📲
    Google Meet

    Classes are conducted via Google Meet. A free Google account is sufficient. We'll send the class link before each session.

Online FAQ

Questions about online classes

All online classes are conducted via Google Meet. A free Google account is all you need. We send you the class link ahead of each session. No special software purchase is required.

Standard online batches are 2 sessions per week, each 1 hour in duration. Some advanced students attend 2–3 sessions per week based on programme requirements. This is confirmed at enrolment.

For regular training — yes, especially at beginner and intermediate levels. The primary limitation is tactile correction (the teacher cannot physically adjust your position). However, Guru Deepa's eye for detail means verbal and visual corrections through video are remarkably precise. Many of our online students have progressed excellently through regular practice.

Programme events such as Salangai Pooja and Arangetram require in-person participation — these are not conducted as online-only performances. When an online student reaches the level of readiness, arrangements for in-person programme participation will be discussed and planned individually with the Guru.

Online classes follow the same curriculum as in-person classes. Sessions are live via Google Meet and are 1 hour in duration. You'll learn adavus, compositions, and abhinaya — everything a Chennai student learns. Sessions are one-to-one or very small groups so you still get personal attention.

We currently serve students in India (IST), UK (BST/GMT), and USA (EST/PST). Classes are scheduled to work around your timezone — please contact us with your location and preferred timing and we will do our best to accommodate.

Online fees are discussed individually and may vary based on session frequency and batch format. Please contact us for the current fee structure. All fees are paid in advance per term.
