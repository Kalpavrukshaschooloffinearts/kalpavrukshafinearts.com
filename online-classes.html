Online Bharatanatyam Classes

Live, personal, Kalakshetra-style Bharatanatyam via Google Meet — from anywhere in the world.

Not pre-recorded. Not a course platform.

Live classes. Real learning.

Our online classes are live, one-to-one or small-group sessions directly with Guru Smt. Deepa Sadasivam via Google Meet. You receive the same corrections, the same attention, and the same curriculum as our in-person students in Chennai. Nothing is diluted. Nothing is pre-recorded.

Kalpavruksha has been teaching online students since 2020. We currently have students learning from the USA, UK, and across India. Classes focus on regular training and skill development; programme events (Salangai Pooja, Arangetram) are managed in-person as and when the student is ready.

Getting started

How online classes work

📋

1. Enquire

Fill our contact form or WhatsApp us. Tell us your age, dance experience, and goals. We'll suggest the right programme.

🎯

2. Free trial class

Your first session is completely free — a 60-minute class via Google Meet so you can experience the teaching with no commitment.

📅

3. Schedule your batch

We work with your timezone to find suitable slots. Classes meet 2 sessions per week for most batches. Flexible scheduling for IST, EST, BST and other time zones.

💻

4. Begin learning

All you need is Google Meet, a 6×6 ft clear space, and a device with a camera. We'll handle everything else.

What you need

Technical requirements

  • 📱
    Device with camera

    Laptop, tablet, or smartphone with a front-facing camera. A laptop placed on a low table (so you can see your whole body) works best.

  • 🌐
    Stable internet connection

    Standard broadband or 4G/5G. We need to see your footwork clearly, so a stable connection matters.

  • 📐
    Clear practice space

    At least 6×6 feet with a hard floor. No special flooring required.

  • 🎧
    Headphones or good speakers

    For hearing the nattuvangam (cymbals and vocal) clearly. Wired headphones reduce lag.

  • 📲
    Google Meet

    Classes are conducted via Google Meet. A free Google account is sufficient. We'll send the class link before each session.
