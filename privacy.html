Privacy Policy

Kalpavruksha School of Fine Arts — kalpavrukshafinearts.com

Last updated: May 2025  |  Effective from: May 2025
We respect your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

1. Who We Are

Kalpavruksha School of Fine Arts ("we", "us", "our") is a Bharatanatyam dance school operating in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, and providing online classes to students in India and abroad. We are reachable at:

  • Email: info@kalpavrukshafinearts.com
  • Phone: +91 72000 95585
  • Address: Kodambakkam, Chennai – 600 024, Tamil Nadu, India

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information when you interact with our website, enquiry forms, or contact us directly:

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth (for enrolment purposes), guardian name (for minors)
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, postal address, city/country
  • Enrolment data: Course interest, preferred batch timings, dance experience
  • Communications: Messages, feedback, or queries you send to us via email, WhatsApp, or the contact form
  • Usage data: Browser type, pages visited, time spent, IP address — collected automatically via Google Analytics when you visit our website

We do not collect payment card details directly. Any fee payments are handled through separate channels and are not stored on this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and arrange trial classes or enrolments
  • To communicate information about batches, schedules, events, and programmes
  • To send occasional updates about the school (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it (via Google Analytics)
  • To comply with any applicable legal or regulatory obligations

We will not use your information for any purpose incompatible with the above without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR — for EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent: When you submit an enquiry or contact form, you consent to us using your information to respond and follow up.
  • Legitimate interests: We have a legitimate interest in communicating with prospective and enrolled students and in improving our website.
  • Legal obligation: Where required to comply with applicable law.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by writing to us at info@kalpavrukshafinearts.com.

5. Applicability of Indian Law (IT Act 2000 & DPDP Act 2023)

As an Indian entity, we are subject to the Information Technology Act, 2000 and its associated rules, including the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011. We apply reasonable security practices to protect sensitive personal data.

We also recognise the provisions of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), which is being implemented in phases by the Government of India. We are committed to aligning our data practices with the obligations under the DPDP Act as they come into force, including:

  • Obtaining clear, informed consent before processing personal data
  • Using data only for the purposes for which consent was given
  • Honouring data principal rights (access, correction, erasure, grievance redressal)
  • Applying appropriate safeguards for the personal data of children and minors

6. Children's Privacy

A significant number of our students are minors (below 18 years of age). We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Personal data of minors is collected from and with the consent of their parent or legal guardian
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental authorisation
  • We do not share or disclose children's personal data to any third party except as necessary for the operation of classes (e.g., online class platforms)
  • Photos or videos of students (including minors) taken at events, recitals, or programmes will be used only with prior parent/guardian consent

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party tools such as Google Workspace, Google Meet, and Google Analytics that we use to operate the school and website. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: If required to disclose information by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Protection of rights: To protect the safety, rights, or property of the school, our students, or others.

8. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our pages. Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your browser — to collect anonymised usage data. This helps us improve the website experience.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party retargeting services.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Enquiry data is typically retained for up to 12 months. Enrolment and student records are retained for the duration of the student's association with the school and a reasonable period thereafter, unless a deletion request is made.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data where it is no longer necessary or where consent has been withdrawn
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format (where technically applicable)
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: At any time, for processing based on your consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@kalpavrukshafinearts.com. We will respond within a reasonable time (generally within 30 days).

If you are in the EEA or UK and are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Facebook, YouTube, Instagram). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

14. Contact & Grievance Redressal

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact:

Kodambakkam, Chennai – 600 024, Tamil Nadu, India
Email: info@kalpavrukshafinearts.com
