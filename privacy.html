Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you interact with this website. By accessing or using the website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, billing/shipping address.
  • Payment Information: Used to process orders securely through third-party payment gateways.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data via cookies or similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver orders.
  • To send transactional communications such as order updates or alerts.
  • To respond to customer enquiries or service requests.
  • To improve website functionality, services, and user experience.
  • For marketing purposes (only with your explicit consent).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share necessary information with third-party service providers such as payment gateways (Razorpay), delivery partners, or IT service providers only to fulfil your order or maintain the website. Personal information may be disclosed if required by law or legal proceedings.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online transmission is 100% secure. You acknowledge this risk when using the site.

Cookies are used to personalise your experience, analyse site traffic, and provide relevant content. This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) for traffic analysis, which may set cookies. You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings, although this may affect site functionality.

6. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

7. Your Rights

8. Refund Policy

If a refund is approved, you will be automatically refunded to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the website after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
