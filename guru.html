Biography

Smt. Deepa Sadasivam is a classically trained Bharatanatyam artist and teacher in the Kalakshetra bani — the tradition established by Rukmini Devi Arundale. She completed her Diploma in Fine Arts (Bharatanatyam) at the Rukmini Devi College of Fine Arts, Kalakshetra Foundation, Thiruvanmiyur, Chennai (2010–2014), followed by an M.A. in Bharatanatyam from the University of Madras (2014–2016) and an M.Phil in Bharatanatyam from Tamil Nadu Open University, Thanjavur (2018–2019).

In 2014, she founded Kalpavruksha School of Fine Arts in Kodambakkam, Chennai — named after the mythical wish-fulfilling tree. Since its founding, the school has grown to 200+ students, spanning children from age 4 through adult learners and online students in India, the UK, and the USA.

Guru Deepa is currently deepening her own practice under the guidance of Smt. Sudharshini Iyer, continuing the classical tradition of a student always remaining a student.

Teaching Philosophy

Guru Deepa’s approach is rooted in the belief that Bharatanatyam is not merely a performing art but a living spiritual practice. The Kalakshetra bani — with its emphasis on devotional purity, sculptural precision, and adherence to the Natyashastra — forms the foundation of every class.

She teaches students of all ages and levels with equal commitment: from a four-year-old encountering their first adavu to an adult discovering the art form for the first time, to advanced students preparing for Arangetram. The class environment is disciplined, warm, and demanding in the best sense.

A key conviction: a true teacher never stops learning. Guru Deepa continues her own training under Smt. Sudharshini Iyer, modelling for her students the humility and dedication that classical training requires across a lifetime.

She has also conducted workshops integrating dance with arithmetic and geometry for children — demonstrating that the structured, rhythmic nature of Bharatanatyam supports cognitive development in ways that extend far beyond the dance classroom.

Kalakshetra Lineage

Guru Deepa is part of the unbroken Kalakshetra tradition founded by Rukmini Devi Arundale in 1936. Her dance gurus include: Smt. Latha Santhanagopalan, Smt. Subhashree Anirudhan, Smt. Vasundhara Thomas, Shri Girish Madhu, Shri K.P. Rakesh, Smt. Bragha Bessel, and Shri Sheejith Krishna. She is currently learning from Smt. Sudharshini Iyer.
