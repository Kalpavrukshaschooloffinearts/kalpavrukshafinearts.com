About us

A living lineage of classical Bharatanatyam

Founded in 2014 by Smt. Deepa Sadasivam, a Kalakshetra Foundation alumna. Like the mythical Kalpavruksha — the wish-fulfilling tree — this school nourishes every student’s artistic aspirations.

We follow the Kalakshetra bani — defined by devotional purity, sculptural precision, and adherence to the Natyashastra.

  • ✦
    Authentic lineage — Direct transmission of Kalakshetra style
  • ✦
    All ages welcome — Children from age 4 · Adults of any age
  • ✦
    Online worldwide — Live classes via Google Meet
  • ✦
    30+ Salangai Poojas · 4+ Arangetrams guided since 2014
