About us

A living lineage of
classical Bharatanatyam

Kalpavruksha School of Fine Arts was founded in 2014 by Smt. Deepa Sadasivam, an alumna of the renowned Kalakshetra Foundation, Chennai. Like the mythical Kalpavriksha — the wish-fulfilling tree — this school exists to nourish every student's deepest artistic aspirations.

We follow the Kalakshetra bani — a style defined by its devotional purity, sculptural precision, and adherence to the Natyashastra. Students range from young children taking their first steps in classical dance, to adults fulfilling a lifelong dream, to dedicated artists preparing for their Arangetram.
