Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Devotion

Established

2014

Kodambakkam, Chennai

Sole Proprietorship UDYAM Registered Kalakshetra Tradition Online & In-Person

Kalpavruksha School of Fine Arts was founded in 2014 in Chennai with a singular purpose — to preserve and transmit the classical art of Bharatanatyam in its authentic Kalakshetra tradition, while nurturing every student's individual artistic voice.

The name Kalpavruksha — the celestial wish-fulfilling tree — reflects our belief that the fine arts are the greatest gift one can bestow upon a student, nurturing not just skill but character, discipline, and a lifelong relationship with culture.

"Bharatanatyam is not merely a dance — it is a moving sculpture, a visual prayer, a language that speaks what words cannot."

Rooted in the Kalakshetra tradition and guided by an experienced Guru, our school welcomes students from Chennai and across the world. We offer training from foundational adavus to full Arangetram — both in-person and online.

Kalpavruksha is a registered Sole Proprietorship firm with UDYAM Registration, committed to the highest standards of classical arts education.

Our Guru

Meet the Founder

DS

Smt. Deepa Sadasivam

Founder & Principal Guru · Bharatanatyam

Kalakshetra Alumna M.A. Bharatanatyam M.Phil. Bharatanatyam 12+ Years Teaching Board Grade Examiner Kalakshetra Outreach Faculty

Smt. Deepa Sadasivam is a distinguished alumna of the Kalakshetra Foundation, Chennai — India's foremost centre for classical arts — where she received rigorous training in the Rukmini Devi tradition of Bharatanatyam.

She holds both a Master of Arts (M.A.) and a Master of Philosophy (M.Phil.) in Bharatanatyam, bringing scholarly depth alongside her performance and teaching practice. With over 12 years of dedicated teaching experience, she has shaped hundreds of students — from beginners stepping into adavus for the first time to accomplished performers gracing the Arangetram stage.

Smt. Deepa currently serves as faculty in the Kalakshetra Outreach Programme and is an empanelled Board Grade Examiner, reflecting the trust the academic community places in her expertise.
