Our history

Highlights from 10 years

A selection of milestones from a decade of teaching, performing, and celebrating Bharatanatyam

2014

Kalpavruksha founded

Smt. Deepa Sadasivam establishes Kalpavruksha School of Fine Arts in Kodambakkam, Chennai, with the vision of bringing authentic Kalakshetra-style Bharatanatyam to a new generation.

2015

First Salangai Pooja

The school conducts its inaugural Salangai Pooja ceremony, honouring the first cohort of students who completed their foundational training.

2017

First Arangetrams

Three students of Kalpavruksha perform their Arangetrams — their classical solo debuts — marking a major milestone for the school. Guru Deepa serves as nattuvanar for all three performances.

2019

Margazhi festival debut

For the first time, Kalpavruksha students perform at Chennai's Margazhi festival season — a landmark recognition of the school's artistic standards.

2020

Online classes launched

Kalpavruksha begins live online Zoom classes, expanding its reach to students in Singapore, UAE, the UK, and the USA. Online Arangetrams are guided successfully.

2021

Bridge Margazhi Festival

Students perform at the Bridge Margazhi Festival 2021, with a full Bharatanatyam concert by a student of Guru Deepa Sadasivam — streamed live to audiences worldwide.

2024

Ten years of Kalpavruksha

The school celebrates its 10th year, having guided over 200 students through their Bharatanatyam journeys and successfully completed over 50 Arangetrams.
